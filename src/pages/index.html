Paul Clelland Accountancy

I provide professional, affordable accountancy and financial management services to charitable, voluntary and social enterprise organisations in Scotland.

About

I am a chartered accountant helping to create a charitable sector in Scotland where trustees take pride in understanding and managing the charitable assets under their stewardship.

If you need support to plan and manage your organisations finances, please get in touch for an informal chat to see how I could help you.

Services

Charity Accounts

  • full year end accounts preparation service
  • independent examination of charity accounts
  • Receipts & Payments and Accruals accounts
  • submission of annual returns and accounts to the Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator (OSCR), and other regulators (eg Companies House) as required
  • meeting requirements of Scottish charity law

Charity Financial Management

  • bookkeeping provision and support
  • advice on bookkeeping systems, including set-up and training
  • advice on payroll provision
  • preparation of cash flow forecasts, budgets and funding applications
  • periodic management accounts and reporting
  • in-house oversight and management of finance function
  • advice on corporation tax, VAT, payroll taxes and other tax issues for charities

Charity Trustee Support

  • advice and guidance on all aspects of charity governance
  • advice on setting up a charity, or closing one down
  • training on the roles and responsibilities of charity trustees
  • Company Secretarial services
